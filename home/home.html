Primary Features

Question Bank and storage

User-friendly Interface

The online examination system should have an intuitive and easy interface for both administrators and students.

Online Submission and Grading

Students can submit their answers online, and the system should facilitate automatic grading for objective questions.

Timer and Progress

An integrated timer helps students manage their time and progress tracking shows the remaining time and completed/remaining questions.
